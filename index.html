Movie Recommendation system

I created a movie buddy that knows what you like! It uses special tools to look at what you've watched and suggests new movies just for you. With a big database of movies, it's like having a personal movie expert. I get to give you recommendations, help you find new favorites, and explore new genres. It's like having your own movie adventure!

My Process: Building a Movie Recommendation System
I started by collecting a vast movie dataset from Kaggle, which became the backbone of my system. Next, I employed NLP techniques and Count Vectorizer to analyze user preferences and viewing history. I then trained machine learning algorithms to generate personalized movie recommendations. After refining my model, I built a user-friendly web interface for easy navigation. Through rigorous testing and iteration, I fine-tuned my system to provide accurate and exciting movie suggestions.

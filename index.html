My Story

I completed my undergraduate degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering in 2017. After graduation, I joined Cyient as a Software Design Engineer, where I worked extensively with large datasets related to transportation schematic components. This role introduced me to the power of data and automation in solving complex engineering problems.

A pivotal moment in my career came when I witnessed the transformative impact of code developed by my tech lead - Excel VBA macros that reduced work from hours to seconds. This experience sparked my fascination with how intelligent automation could revolutionize workflows and solve real-world challenges at scale.

My interest in AI deepened as I began working on prediction models, seeing firsthand how machine learning could extract insights from complex datasets. This inspired me to pursue a Master's in Information Technology in 2024, where I formalized my understanding of AI/ML concepts and techniques.

Following my academic journey, I worked as a Machine Learning Fellow at Fellowship AI, where I learned fascinating things about AI agents and their applications. This experience exposed me to cutting-edge developments in the field and reinforced my passion for building intelligent systems that can adapt and learn.

Currently, I'm working as an AI Python Engineer at Narwal, where I'm applying my skills across diverse domains and technical challenges. This role allows me to see AI's transformative potential across different industries.

I'm passionate about building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems and am always excited to discuss challenging projects, emerging technologies, and innovative applications of AI/ML.
